About Us Activity Time is a web platform for creating and sharing your activities. Within ActivityTime you will be able to save your private daily activities, create personal group activities by sharing them with your friends, or make an activity public and accessible to anyone who wants, with the "Activity Zone" module that allows you to do real-time searches of public activities present by area, so you can know opportunities around you and take part immediately. welcome!
